Join the Peloton! Ride, Run, or Skate for Divestment!

Sign up to ride: https://tinyurl.com/bike-divest

Join us for the first ever Tour de Divestment: a cycling, skating, running, walking, whatever, event on Cornell's Ithaca campus. You can join as a participant or a spectator. Races will be available for all skill levels and interests. Participants should arrive by 12:45 pm, and spectators are welcome to come between 12:45 to 1:30 and beyond.
